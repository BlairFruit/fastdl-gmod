-- POEMS --

Sometimes, I wonder what it would be like to be someone else.
Would I be happy?
Would I be sad?
Would I be a better me?
Would I be a worse me?
I would rather never find out.

End


That man, the one behind the screen.
Told me to keep looking beyond what I wanted.
He said that if I kept looking, I would find something better.
I kept looking.
And I never could go back.

End


A figure appeared to me once,
And it told me "I have a deal you cannot refuse."
I followed it, not knowing where it would lead.
And now, I am just a shell of a person, wandering through the void.

End


Mama said that writing a diary helps you reflect on your thoughts and feelings.
But I realized that it was the only thing that was holding me from my verdict.
He calls for me, and I cannot say no.
It was meant to be a goodbye a long time ago

End
